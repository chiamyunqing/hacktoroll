Where to begin?

To explore the web page, the most important tool that you will need is the browser developer tool. Visit this to learn how to begin your experiential learning!

Challenge #1: The Gatekeeper

As the clue said, start inspecting! Right click the page to view the page source. Looking through the html code, you can easily spot the username that is hidden as a meta data in the header. You can check out here to learn more about meta tag.The password can be found as a html comment which begins with <! –– and closes with ––>.

Challenge #2: The Fortune Teller

The picture is indeed valuable. Upon clicking the picture, it will open a new tab which has a cookie named 'fortune'. Right click the page to inspect and from the developer console, go to the Applications tab. Expand the Cookies dropdown under the Storage section and submit the value of the cookie named 'fortune' to get through this challenge. To learn more about cookies, check this out!

Challenge #3: Dora the Explorer

You are now at Challenge 3 and the picture shows that the next destination you should be at is ro11ingind4deep. So just follow the path and append 'ro11ingin4deep' to the url! Visit this to learn more about the components of a URL.

Challenge #4: Get Outta Here

Just like how the chameleon changed its colour, change the colour of the logout button by editing the HTML. Right click the page to inspect and in the Elements tab, find which part of the HTML has the anchor tag which begins with <a> and closes with </a>. Change the style property, color from #33cc33 to any colour you like (e.g. white or blue like the chameleon). Click the logout button to complete the last challenge of HackToRoll and arrive at this Solutions page!

HackToRoll

HackToRoll is a fun and educational website meant for beginners to learn about web pages independently.

"Learning is experience. Everything else is just information." - Albert Einstein

Start Hacking!

What is HackToRoll?

HackToRoll consists of four challenges that aim to give some insights on web pages through experiential learning.

How It Started

This project is developed for Hack&Roll 2021. As beginners ourselves in web development, we took online courses to upskill ourselves. During the process, we felt that there could be more fun online resources to encourage people to explore the internal workings of webpages and that is how HackToRoll began.

Mission

To inspire amateurs to take the leap and explore.

Future Plans

As this is our first hackathon, we are always looking to improve. We welcome any ideas to improve and extend the challenges.
